Overview

The gorgeous 50 Sundancer features large hull windows and graceful hull & deck lines, an enclosed cockpit Sunroom with two retractable sunroofs and a pivoting double helm seat for enhanced sociability. The mid master indulges owners with new fabric and finish packages, and separate head and shower compartments. A gourmet galley and plush sofa round out the rewards in the main cabin in a naturally-lit, atrium type environment.

KEY FEATURES

  • Companion bench with molded fiberglass storage.
  • Custom euro-style helm seat with flip-up, thigh-rise bolster, armrests, and heavy-duty pedestal with slide, swivel and pneumatic vertical adjustment, and fiberglass seat shell.
  • Cockpit wet bar with sink and faucet, solid-surface countertop, stainless-steel handrail, trash receptacle, and storage cabinet.
  • Electrically actuated engine access hatch.
  • Stereo with Wireless Bluetooth and media inputs
  • Innerspring mattress standard in master stateroom.
  • Optional Zeus® propulsion with helm joystick & Skyhook® station keeper
  • Standard Twin Cummins® QSC 550 HO Diesel V-Drive Inboards w/DTS
  • Standard generator with sound shield.
  • Fiberglass hardtop with full enclosure
