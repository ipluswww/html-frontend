Overview

No Boundaries
Get ready for the Viking 82 Cockpit Motor Yacht

Capitalizing on the success of the Viking 75 Motor Yacht, it was understandable that the Viking design group would move forward with a series of larger models. The Viking 82 Cockpit Motor Yacht is the beginning of this new wave.

The Viking 82 CMY provides a seamless transition from a luxurious, high-performance cruiser to a blue-water yacht fisherman creating the ideal platform for tournament fishing one day and heading on a three week vacation the next. The 108 square foot cockpit accessed from dual teak-planked staircases on the after deck features an inviting arena for fishing, diving, paddle boarding and other fun water activities. Outfitted with a comely observation mezzanine topped with comfortable Sunbrella cushions, it is bound to be the choice gathering spot for family and guests to relax and enjoy their memorable days on the water.

The nine-foot wide lounge molded into the mezzanine features a trove of useful amenities. Port and starboard stowage compartments serve as tackle and gear wells. An engineroom access door is built into mezzanine backrest. A companionway leads into the crew quarters. Chill boxes for drinks and bait are located in the port and starboard staircases. A molded transom fishbox doubles as a convenient stowage bin for masks, fins and other dive gear. A walk-through transom door with a lift gate complements the molded teak planked swim platform.

On the main deck, sophisticated styling merges with sociability. Forward and aft deck lounges capture the outdoor spirit. Inside the yacht, eight tons of chilled water air conditioning provides a climate controlled environment where a lavish walnut salon, formal and casual dining areas, and a world class galley set a festive mood. Below, four stunningly appointed staterooms, including an amazing full beam master suite abound with substance and elegance. Our interior design team assures each and every one of your needs is delivered to your utmost satisfaction and personalization.

Available with an open bridge or an enclosed bridge, each version demonstrates a unique path to boundless opportunities. The open bridge layout is all about the great outdoors with a centerline helm forward flanked with casual seating areas. Abaft the helm, a family size dinette, and a wet bar with refrigeration, a sink and an electric grill get the party started. Further aft there is a sun pad, room for lounge chairs, and a teak planked staircase to the lower after deck. The climate controlled enclosed bridge showcases an impressive helm layout and a lounge area aft creating the atmosphere of a second salon with plush seating an entertainment center. A sliding glass door opens to the aft deck with available lounges, a wet bar and dinghy stowage.

Outfitted with custom designed navigation and entertainment systems provided by our subsidiary Atlantic Marine Electronics, the 82 CMY can be delivered turnkey ready. This new Viking motor yacht awaits your inspection and command. We encourage you to contact your Viking dealer for more information about these exciting additions to the Viking fleet.
